The Learning Curve Is the Moat

08
What The Winners Do

The Adoption Playbook

Months 1–4: Give Everyone Access

Universal AI tools. Governance from day one. 10–15 AI Champions across departments. Pair junior (AI-savvy) with senior (domain judgment).

Months 4–10: Rebuild Workflows

Pick 3–5 high-value workflows: proposals, DD review, reporting. Build domain-specific prompt libraries. Measure experimentation, not just output.

Months 10–18: Enterprise AI

RAG-powered knowledge engine on your project history. AI-enhanced services. Evolve from time-based billing to value-based pricing.

The Golden Rule

Budget 70% for people & process, 30% for technology. And the CEO must use it visibly — that single act determines adoption more than any mandate.
